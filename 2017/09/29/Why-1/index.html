Why Whole Lotta Mamma???

2017-09-29
#Whole 30 #Motivation #Thoughts

It all starts with why

“I have come to believe that caring for myself is not self-indulgent. Caring for myself is an act of survival.”

Audre Lorde

My life changed beyond measure when we welcomed our beautiful daughter into the world. It was such an honor to keep her safe and growing big and strong prior to her birth. Along with her growth, this Mamma also saw some pretty substantial growth. While some was neccesary, I think some can be attributed to the steady diet of ice cream along with cheese and pickle sandwiches that seemed to sit best in my tummy. After Baby D’s birth, I saw a quick drop in quite a few pounds. I clung to the hope that breastfeeding would take care of the rest, and it would be no time before I would be fitting back into my post pregnancy pants. What I hadn’t factored in was the lack of time for self care after becoming a new Mamma. Not to mention the heavy dependence on sugar to survive the incredible sleep deprivation. So here I am- additcted to sugar, feeling sluggish, lacking clothes that fit and alltogether not feeling awesome about myself. And while this is where I currently am, I recognize it’s not where I ultimately want to be. I want to focus on moving from being a whole lot of Mamma, to a whole Mamma. To invest time and energy into caring for myself through the foods I eat and the actions I take. To show myself the love I know I deserve. I recognize that this is not just for me, but to show my beautiful daughter the value and need to love and care for herself. Join me on my quest to be a whole Mamma.
