Whole 30 Preparation

2017-09-30
#Whole 30 #Motivation

“By failing to prepare, you are preparing to fail”

Benjamin Franklin

Food and I- we are in a bit of an unhealthy relationship these days. Because of this, I have decided to complete the Whole 30 program. While I wish I could say I could kick this sugar habit without a program, let’s be honest, that craving for an afternoon coffee and cookie is just soooooo strong. I am really looking forward to dedicating 30 days to focusing on eating yummy, whole foods and hopefully resetting my mindset and relationship with food. Because I know this will be such a shift for me, we did a major cleanout of the cupboards this past weekend to remove any tempting food. Here is a sneak peak at what the cupboards look like after removing all of our non Whole 30 compliant foods.

I have my spreadsheet all set with all of our meals for the week, the grocery list has been checked and rechecked, and I am ready to rock this out. I am certain this won’t be a super easy journey, but nonetheless, I am feeling excited. It has really started to hit me, especially with Baby D now sitting with us at the table in her high chair, the impact that our choices have on her. She is watching EVERYTHING. And while yours truly has always struggled in maintaining a healthy relationship with food, I am determined to not pass this mindset down.

So ready, set and let’s go!
